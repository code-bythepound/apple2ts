Aztec Controls

W: walk
R: run
J: jump
S: stop
C: climb
A: turn left
D: turn right
G: crawl (G to move)
P: place and light explosive
T: take item
O: opens box or dig in trash
L: look in box
Z: inventory

F: goes to fight mode:
   S: spin around
   A: move one to left
   D: move one to right
   L: lunge with machete
   M: strike down with machete
   G: draw fun
   Space: shoot gun
